Energy Decoupling Analysis

Which countries have grown their economies while reducing energy intensity? Decoupling means GDP energy intensity falls even as per-capita energy use stays stable or rises.

Strong decoupling (GDP intensity ↓ >20%, per-capita stable/↑)
Weak decoupling (GDP intensity ↓, per-capita ↓ less)
No decoupling (both rising or flat)
Recoupling (GDP intensity ↑)
